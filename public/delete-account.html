Delete Your Budget App Account

Account and data deletion instructions

Delete your account in the app

  1. Sign in to Budget App.
  2. Open Profile.
  3. Select Delete my account.
  4. Enter your current password, type DELETE, and confirm.

If you cannot sign in

Email privacy@funmilayotobun.com from the address registered to your Budget App account. Use the subject “Budget App account deletion request.” We may ask you to verify control of the account before processing the request.

What is deleted

Deletion removes your login, password hash, sessions, invitations tied to your account, transactions, monthly budgets, income records, fixed expenses, debts and payment history, salary cycles, savings goals and contributions, and reminder-delivery records linked directly to your account.

What may be retained

Limited records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal compliance. Any retained information is restricted to those purposes and deleted when no longer needed.
